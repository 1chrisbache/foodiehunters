The process

From scattered reviews to a clear path forward

01

We gather every public review and rating from Google, Yelp, TripAdvisor, DoorDash, Facebook, Yahoo Local, GrubHub, and Instagram — building a complete picture of how the internet sees your restaurant today.

02

We cross-reference your performance across all platforms, identify where your rankings lag your actual quality, and surface recurring complaint themes that are quietly dragging down your scores.
