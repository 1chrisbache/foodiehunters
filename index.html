The process

From scattered reviews to a clear path forward

We gather every public review and rating from Google, Yelp, TripAdvisor, DoorDash, Facebook, Yahoo Local, GrubHub, and Instagram — building a complete picture of how the internet sees your restaurant today.

02

We cross-reference your performance across all platforms, identify where your rankings lag your actual quality, and surface recurring complaint themes that are quietly dragging down your scores.

Every platform, one report

Most owners only look at Yelp and Google. The biggest gaps almost always live somewhere else.

Google
Primary local search. Photos, Q&A, and response cadence all influence the algorithm.
Yelp
Most trusted by local diners. Ranking signals differ significantly from Google.
TripAdvisor
Critical in Vegas — tourists plan meals here. Often the largest gap for quality restaurants.
DoorDash
Delivery ratings diverge from dine-in. Packaging and menu curation need separate attention.
Facebook
Discovery channel for the 35–55 demographic. Frequently abandoned by independent restaurants.
Instagram
Visual identity and follower-to-review ratio. A low ratio signals untapped reach.
Yahoo Local
Pulls traffic from older demographics and Safari users. Often overlooked entirely.
GrubHub
Delivery sentiment and pricing perception — separate from the in-house experience.
